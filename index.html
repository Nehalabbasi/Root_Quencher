About Our product

Innovative Subsurface Watering
And Irrigation System At Its Best

Root Quencher is an underground watering device that delivers water to where trees and shrubs need it most: the roots. This approach eliminates evaporation from surface watering, eliminating water waste by up to 50%. It can connect to existing sprinkler systems or a hose for automated watering
